## Jawaban

### Pertanyaan 18

1. $$\begin{array}{rcl}
  \text{1 KB}& = & 10.000 \\\\
   10^3 & = & 10.000 \\\\
   1.000& \ne & 10.000
  \end{array}$$
  
  Jawabannya salah
  
2. $$\begin{array}{rcl}
  \text{10 PB}&=&10^{16}\\\\
  10 \times 10^{15}&=& 10^{16}\\\\
  10^{1+15}&=&10^{16}\\\\
  10^{16}&=&10^{16}
  \end{array}$$
  Jawaban benar
  
3. $$\begin{array}{rcl}
  \text{100 TB}&=&10^{14}\\\\
  100 \times 10^{12} &=&10^{14} \\\\
  10^2\times 10^{12} &=&10^{14}\\\\
  10^{2+12}&=&10^{14}\\\\
  10^{14}&=&10^{14}
  \end{array} $$
  
  Jawaban benar
  

### Pertanyaan 19

1. $$\begin{array}{rcl}
  \sqrt{(\text{1 GB})}&=&2^{15}\\\\
  \sqrt{(2^{30}})&=&2^{15}\\\\
  (2^{30})^\frac{1}{2} &=& 2^{15}\\\\
  2^{30\times \frac{1}{2}}&=&2^{15}\\\\
  2^{15}&=&2^{15}
  \end{array} $$
  Jawaban benar
  
2. $$\begin{array}{rcl}
  \sqrt{(\text{4 GB})}&=&2\times 10^6 \\\\
  \sqrt{4\times 10^9}&=&2\times 10^6\\\\
  \sqrt{4}\times \sqrt{10^9} &=& 2\times 10^6\\\\
  2\times 10^{\frac{9}{2}} & \ne& 2 \times 10^6 
  \end{array} $$
  Jawaban salah
  
3. $$\begin{array}{rcl}
  (\sqrt{(\text{1 GB})})^2&=&2^{30}\\\\
  (\sqrt{2^{30}})^2&=&2^{30}\\\\
  2^{(30\times 2\times \frac{1}{2})}&=&2^{30}\\\\
  2^{30}&=&2^{30}
  \end{array} $$
